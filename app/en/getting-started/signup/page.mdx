
# Create Account

You can easily sign up for Taskel with your Google account.

1. Access [Taskel App](https://taskel.app).
2. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button.
3. Select the account you want to use on the Google authentication screen.
4. Once authentication is complete, you will be automatically redirected to the dashboard.
