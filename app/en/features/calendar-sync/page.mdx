
# Google Calendar Sync

Import Google Calendar events into Taskel and manage them centrally as tasks.

1. Open the **Settings** screen.
2. Select the **"Integrations"** tab.
3. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button and allow access to your calendar.
4. Once integration is complete, calendar events will automatically appear on Taskel's timeline.
5. **Sync Button**: If you want to sync manually, click the update button at the top right of the screen.
