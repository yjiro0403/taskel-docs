## Daily View (Recommended)
- **Shortcut**: `D`
- **Use Case**: Main screen for executing today's tasks.
- **Feature**: Focus on the tasks at hand while being conscious of the flow of time.

![Daily View](/images/daily-view.png)

## Weekly View
- **Shortcut**: `W`
- **Use Case**: Check and adjust this week's schedule.
- **Feature**: Get a bird's-eye view of task load for the week.

![Weekly View](/images/weekly-view.png)

## Monthly View
- **Shortcut**: `M`
- **Use Case**: Check monthly events and deadlines.
- **Feature**: Grasp the overall schedule in a calendar format.

![Monthly View](/images/monthly-view.png)

## Yearly View
- **Shortcut**: `Y`
- **Use Case**: Set annual goals and major milestones.
- **Feature**: Have a long-term perspective throughout the year.

![Yearly View](/images/yearly-view.png)

## Planning View
- **Shortcut**: `P`
- **Use Case**: Organize unsorted tasks (Inbox) and assign dates.
- **Feature**: Inbox on the right, calendar on the left. Plan by drag & drop.

![Planning View](/images/planning-view.png)
