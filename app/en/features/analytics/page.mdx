
# Analytics

Visualize your activity data to gain insights for improvement.
(Access from **"Analytics"** in the sidebar)

## Trends

- Check trends in completed tasks and total working time with graphs.
- objectively judge "Did I work harder than last week?" or "Am I working too much?".

## Breakdown

- Check time allocation by project or tag with pie charts.
- Useful for discovering bottlenecks like "Which project am I spending too much time on?".
