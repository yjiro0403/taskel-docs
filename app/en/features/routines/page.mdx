## Creating a Routine

1. Select the **"Routines"** menu from the sidebar.
2. Click the `New Routine` button.
3. Set the **Frequency**.
    - **Daily**: Every day (specific days of the week possible)
    - **Weekly**: Specific day every week
    - **Monthly**: Specific day every month
4. Set details like time, project, and tags, then save.

## Virtual Tasks and Materialization

When you set a routine, it appears faintly as a **"Virtual Task"** in Daily View.

Virtual tasks are converted to **"Real Tasks"** at the following times, and can then be operated like normal tasks:

1. **When the day arrives**: Automatically materialized when you open Daily View
2. **When you edit it**: Materialized when you click the virtual task and modify its content
3. **When you press the Play button**: Materialized when you start time tracking
