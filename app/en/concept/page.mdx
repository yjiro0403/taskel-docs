
# Concept

> **Goals and today, always connected.**

**Turn your year into today's 30 minutes.**

Taskel is not a "task management tool."
It's an app that provides the experience of **bidirectionally connecting your annual goals with today's actions, running a daily cycle of execution, measurement, and reflection, so that both your goals and yourself are continuously refined**.
