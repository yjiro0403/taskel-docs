
# Taskel Documentation

> **Goals and today, always connected.**

**Turn your year into today's 30 minutes.**

Taskel is not a "task management tool."
It's an app that provides the experience of **bidirectionally connecting your annual goals with today's actions, running a daily cycle of execution, measurement, and reflection, so that both your goals and yourself are continuously refined**.

## Design Philosophy

### Assist, not Delegate
AI is a "mirror." It doesn't estimate for you or change your priorities. You decide, so you grow.

### Incomplete is Not Punishment
Chute (postponement) is an official feature. Freedom from the "Overdue" red-letter hell.

### Goals Are Living Things
Changing doesn't mean failing â€” changing means growing. We welcome goal evolution.
